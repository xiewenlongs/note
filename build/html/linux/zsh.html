oh-my-zsh¶

好用的插件¶

要启用zsh的插件， 在~/.zshrc 中设置:

plugins=(git autojump last-working-dir tmux tmuxinator web-search fancy-ctrl-z themes)
  • autojump
  • last-working-dir
  • tmux
  • tmuxinator
  • web-search
  • fancy-ctrl-z

升级oh-my-zsh¶

要升级oh-my-zsh, 终端输入:

> upgrade_oh_my_zsh
